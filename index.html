Profitable App Profiles for the App Store and Google Play Market

In this Jupyter Notebook project, I delve into an analysis of Android and iOS mobile apps utilising datasets sourced from Kaggle. The objective is to discern the characteristics of apps that are prone to garnering more users. This analysis serves to aid a hypothetical company in making informed decisions regarding which types of apps to develop within these markets.

The analysis primarily employs fundamental Python techniques, abstaining from external libraries. Through a combination of Python basics such as arithmetic operations, variable handling, and common data types, alongside more advanced concepts like list and for loops, conditional statements, dictionaries, and frequency tables, I construct a comprehensive understanding of the dataset. Additionally, the project showcases the implementation of functions to streamline the analytical process.

This project is presented in the form of a Jupyter Notebook, providing transparency into the analysis methodology and facilitating ease of replication and further exploration.

Exploring Hacker News Posts

This project is an exploration of submissions made on the Hacker News website. The submissions of interest are the ones that begin with the titles 'Show HN' and 'Ask HN'. 'Show HN' posts are submitted to show the Hacker News community a project, product, or, simply, something interesting. While 'Ask HN' submissions ask the community a specific question.

This project compares these two types of posts to determine:

Do 'Ask HN' or 'Show HN' receive more comments on average? Do posts created at a certain time receive more comments on average?

Exploring eBay Car Sales Data

This project aims to clean and analyse a dataset of used cars from eBay Kleinanzeigen, a classifieds section of the German eBay website. The dataset used for this project is a modified version of the one found online, containing only 50,000 sampled data points. It has been intentionally "dirtied" to simulate a pre-cleaned, scraped dataset.

The main objectives of this project include exploring the data using Pandas and conducting thorough data cleaning procedures. Through these tasks, insights into the characteristics and trends within the used car market on eBay Kleinanzeigen are revealed. This analysis aims to provide valuable insights for individuals or businesses involved in the automotive industry or interested in online classifieds data.

This project showcases proficiency in data exploration techniques with Pandas and demonstrates expertise in data cleaning methodologies, both essential skills in the field of data analysis.
